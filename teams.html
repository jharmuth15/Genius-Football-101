NFL Teams

The NFL (National Football League) is the American football league, and is one of the most popular sports leagues in the world. It consists of 32 teams in 22 states in 31 Cities (New York has 2 franchises). It has 8 total divions divided up by 2 conferences, the Naitonal Football Conference(NFC) and the American Football Conference(AFC).
